Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.
Simple as that.

MacroMate was built with a zero-collection philosophy. We don't want your data — we want you to hit your macros.

01 No Data Collection

MacroMate does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. We have no account system, no analytics pipeline, and no data we could sell even if we wanted to.

02 On-Device Storage Only

Everything you log — your macros, meals, water intake, goals, and streaks — lives entirely on your device. Uninstall the app and it's gone. No cloud, no servers, no trace.

03 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your information to any third parties, advertisers, or data brokers — because there is no information to share.

04 No AI or Tracking

MacroMate contains no machine learning models, behavioral tracking, or usage analytics. Your patterns are your own. The app does not observe or learn from how you use it.

05 Policy Updates

If this privacy policy ever changes in a meaningful way, we will communicate that through an app update notice. The current version of this policy is always available at this URL.

By continuing to use MacroMate, you acknowledge and agree to this privacy policy.
