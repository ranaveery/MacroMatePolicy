Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.
Simple as that.

MacroMate was built with a zero-collection philosophy. I don't want your data — I want you to hit your macros.

01No Data Collection

MacroMate does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There is no account system, no analytics pipeline, and no data that could be sold even if I wanted to.

02On-Device Storage Only

Everything you log — macros, meals, water intake, goals, and streaks — lives entirely on your device. Uninstall the app and it's gone. No cloud, no servers, no trace.

03No Third-Party Sharing

I do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your information to any third parties, advertisers, or data brokers — because there is no information to share.

04No AI or Tracking

MacroMate contains no machine learning models, behavioral tracking, or usage analytics. Your patterns are your own. The app does not observe or learn from how you use it.

05Policy Updates

If this privacy policy ever changes in a meaningful way, it will be communicated through an app update notice. The current version is always available at this URL.

By continuing to use MacroMate, you acknowledge and agree to this privacy policy.
